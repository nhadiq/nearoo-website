* NEAROO TALKIES CREATOR PROGRAM

 

Talkies Creator Program (TCP) allows creators to monetize their Content. Monetization policies are a collection of policies that allows creator to earn revenue out of the content they upload. Program is available to creators who live in a country where TCP is available. Program will not be applicable to a content which have copyright or content related strike.

 

* STAY ACTIVE TO KEEP MAKING MONEY

 

To support creators who are active we may disable monetization on channels that haven’t uploaded video for six months or more.

 

As part of TCP, creators can make good revenue based on parameters listed below

 

·        AED 10 on first and AED 5 for rest of approved videos.

·        AED 5 Fills for each unique view from a country where TCP is available with 100% retention rate on video. If retention rate is less, proportionately amount will be reduced

·        1% commission on value of total purchases made by viewers linked to the items listed in the content

 

Validity: Charted monetization policy is valid until 31st January 2022 or an earlier date after written notice send to the email id of creator.
